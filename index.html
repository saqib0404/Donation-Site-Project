Food for a Family

There are thousands of people out there dying out of hunger. Lets reach out to them. For each family the products we have choosen are 5kg rice, 2kg suger, 2 kg chira, 1 packet salt, some biscuits, 1kg potato, 1kg pulse. For each family the cost is 985/=

First our target is Sylhet, then we will be moving to next places. We are going to build shelters using tin, cement. .There are thousands of people out there living out of home, under no shelter. Lets reach out to them. For each family the cost is 10000/=
